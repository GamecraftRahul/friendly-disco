Condition: Dengue

Overview:
Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral infection.

Causes:
- Aedes mosquito bite

Symptoms:
- High fever
- Severe headache
- Pain behind eyes
- Joint and muscle pain
- Skin rash

Complications:
- Dengue hemorrhagic fever
- Shock

Treatment:
- Hydration
- Paracetamol (avoid NSAIDs)
- Hospital monitoring in severe cases

Emergency Signs:
- Persistent vomiting
- Bleeding gums
- Severe abdominal pain
- Drop in blood pressure
